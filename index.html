My portfolio

I am Ayomide David Sayo-Adeyemi, a passionate and versatile professional with expertise in software engineering, UX/UI design, and graphic design. As a Software Engineering student at the University of Calgary, I have developed strong technical skills in React, Firebase Firestore, and C programming. I enjoy solving real-world problems through code and have built projects like an inventory management system that enhances efficiency and organization. With a resilient approach to project management, I thrive in balancing technical problem-solving with creativity to develop impactful solutions.

Beyond software development, I have a deep passion for UX/UI design, where I focus on creating seamless and user-friendly digital experiences. I prioritize accessibility, usability, and visual appeal to ensure that every product I design is both functional and engaging. My background in software development allows me to bridge the gap between design and implementation, ensuring that user experiences are intuitive, efficient, and visually compelling.

I am also a graphic designer under the alias @_fadedvisuals, where I specialize in cover art, branding, and digital visuals. Through my design work, I bring ideas to life with a unique blend of creativity and storytelling. I have worked with brands, artists, and businesses to craft visuals that not only look great but also resonate deeply with their audiences. My ability to merge technical expertise with artistic vision allows me to create designs that are both innovative and meaningful.

I am always eager to learn, grow, and take on new challenges in the fields of software development, UI/UX design, and digital creativity. My diverse skill set, strong work ethic, and adaptability make me a valuable asset to any forward-thinking team. I am looking for opportunities where I can contribute my skills, collaborate with like-minded professionals, and create impactful digital experiences that make a difference.
